The Full Process

Every step explained — so you know exactly what to expect

1

Contact Us on WhatsApp or via the Form

Reach out directly on WhatsApp or fill in our contact form. Share your assessment type, subject, word count, deadline, and any special instructions. Our team responds within minutes — any time, day or night.

Takes 2 minutes
2

Sign the Formal Agreement

We draw up a clear, transparent agreement covering the scope of work, deliverables, timeline, and expectations. This protects both you and us from the very beginning — no ambiguity.

Signed digitally
3

Choose Your Service Option

You have two options — pick whichever suits you best:

Option 01

You Share Materials

Upload your templates, briefs, and study materials directly. You stay in control throughout the process.

  • You upload templates and study resources
  • We review and start work immediately
  • We deliver via Google Drive for your review
  • You handle the final submission yourself
Option 02

We Access Dashboard

Share your college login and we handle everything — from downloading materials to final submission.

  • Share your college dashboard credentials
  • We download all required materials
  • We deliver to Google Drive for your approval
  • We submit directly on your behalf
Your choice
4

We Review Requirements & Send Invoice

Our expert thoroughly reviews your assessment brief, rubric, and any marking criteria. We then send you a clear, itemised invoice. Once you review and approve, we begin work immediately.

Transparent pricing
5

Expert Completes Your Assessment

Your assigned expert writes the assessment from scratch — 100% human-written, plagiarism-free, and tailored precisely to your requirements. The completed work is uploaded to a shared Google Drive folder.

100% human-written
6

You Review & Request Revisions

Access the completed work on Google Drive, review every section, and request any changes. We revise until you are fully satisfied — unlimited revisions, no extra charges.

Unlimited revisions
7

Final Approval & Submission

Once you approve the final work, submission happens based on your chosen option. Option 1 students receive the final Google Drive link for self-submission. Option 2 students have us submit directly to their college dashboard.

Smooth submission
8

Pay & Share Feedback

After successful submission, we send the final invoice for payment clearance. We then kindly ask you to share your feedback or review — this helps us continue improving and helping more students like you.

Pay after success
