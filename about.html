Who We Are

AssessmentHelp is a professional academic writing service powered by The Student Helpline. We are a team of 1500+ PhD-qualified experts, subject specialists, and academic consultants dedicated to helping students succeed.

Founded in 2018, we have helped over 180,000 students across the UK, Australia, USA, Canada, India, and beyond. Our mission: deliver expert, original, human-written academic support that genuinely helps students understand and excel.

We operate on a unique pay-after-delivery model — because we believe in earning your trust before your payment.
